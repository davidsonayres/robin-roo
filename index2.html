We first hired Brianna as an occasional babysitter almost three years ago for out then two-year old son, Wyatt. He fell absolutely in love and was always so happy to have her come over. She is very engaging and playful, yet also sets firm boundaries and truly respects out family's philosophies. She ensures out children (and our home) are safe and clean. She is also an absolute laundry wizard! Most importantly, she really knows our children as individuals and respects their spirits. We are very grateful to have Brianna helping us raise our boys.
