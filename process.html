OUR PROCESS

Once you have filled out this form, we will contact your family. We will set up an initial interview to better get to know you, your family, and everything you hope to achieve by having a nanny in your home.

At this point, we require a 25% deposit fee of the total agency cost. Further details on costs are listed on our Agency Fees page.
We screen resumes, conduct interviews, and find exceptional nannies who will work and blend well with your family. We know that the nanny-to-family relationship is vital and we work tirelessly to ensure we find you the perfect candidate.
We then send you portfolios of the top candidates and we work with you to set up interviews at your convenience.
If you wish to set up a paid working interview after your initial interview of the candidates (play date, library excursion, park visit, etc) to better get to know your top choice(s), we will work with you to make these arrangements.
You then notify Robin & Roo regarding the candidate you have selected.
We work with your family and your new nanny to establish a contract that reflects the terms of the working arrangement.

Once the contract has been signed by both the family and nanny and the remainder of the agency fee has been paid, the contact information between the nanny and the family is exchanged and your new nanny can begin work!

Please visit our frequently asked questions page for more information.
