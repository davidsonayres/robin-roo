AM I GUARANTEED A BABYSITTER?

While we will do our best to provide you an excellent babysitter, one is not always guaranteed. Giving us as much notice as possible will increase your chances of securing a babysitter, but we will also do our absolute best to accommodate last minute requests.

WHAT IF I NEED TO CANCEL MY BABYSITTER?

We understand that plans change and cancellations may happen. In the event of a cancellation this is our process:

If you cancel 24 hours before the babysitter was to start, only the non-refundable booking fee will be charged.

If you cancel less than 24 hours before the babysitter was to start, the non-refundable agency booking fee and a minimum two hours of childcare fee ($30) will be charged.

CAN I REQUEST AN OVERNIGHT OR TRAVEL NANNY?

Yes! We will do our best to find someone who meets your overnight and travel requirements. Giving Robin and Roo Sitters and Nannies as much notice as possible will greatly increase the possibility of securing such a babysitter.

I DON'T LIVE IN TORONTO. CAN YOU STILL FIND ME A NANNY?

Yes! While we primarily work with families in the Greater Toronto Area, we do have contacts in many cities across Canada. Please fill out the nanny request form and we will be in touch!

WHAT IF MY BABYSITTER CANCELS?

Someone from Robin & Roo will be in touch with you as soon as possible should a babysitter cancel. We will do our absolute best to find you an equally great replacement babysitter. On the rare occasion we are not able to provide a replacement babysitter your booking fee for your next babysitter will be waived.

WHAT IF MY CAREGIVER ISN'T A GOOD FIT?

While we do our best to ensure the sitter we send you will work well with your family, if your babysitter is not a good match we will be sure to discuss with you what the challenges were and send you someone more suitable next time.
In the case of hiring a full or part time nanny, if any aspects of the contract you sign are not fully honoured within the first three months, you will be assigned one new nanny at no extra charge.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO FIND A NANNY?

Typically the process can take between 1-2 weeks to find and hire the perfect candidate. If you need someone sooner we are happy to provide a temporary babysitter for your family while we expedite the process.

HOW MUCH DOES A NANNY COST?
